ABOUT THE BAND

lenanasplit is a 6-piece Vocaloid J-Rock band based in the DMV (DC/Maryland/Virginia) area. We bring Vocaloid vocals to life and are focused on bringing explosive anime-concert energy to the live stage.

We formed in late 2024, and since then we've blended high-octane headbanging with emotional energy at stages like MAGFest and the University of Maryland. Our mission is to bring J-rock hype and hidden-gem tracks to fans across the DMV and the US.

Specializing in iconic Vocaloid hits like Lost One's Weeping, Rolling Girl, and Ghost Rule, plus original J-Rock compositions inspired by ONE OK ROCK, RADWIMPS, and wowaka. Available for venues and events across the East Coast.
